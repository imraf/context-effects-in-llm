Visual Story Tellers — The Power BI Experience Series #1

We are sharing our customer success stories and can help you with our amazing talent in simple Visual Story Telling and insights.

Problem: Customer a leading retailer requested for root cause analysis of job failures in production and do retrospective analysis

Challenge: With over 500 jobs running, multiple categories, groups, technologies, and failures, needed a single snapshot image with a summary and understanding of the failures. So Many Data Items in one page!!!! How do we do it???

Regular Solution: Build a bunch of bar charts, line charts, grids etc?

Visual Story Teller Experience: Our visual story teller experts jumped in and built a decomposition chart which showcased the entire root cause summary in a single visual. A consolidated view of about 6–9 charts in a single landscape view.

Outcome: Customer rejoiced with the one pager summary view with numbers and insights.
